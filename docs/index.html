The problem

You already have the chart.
You still can't defend the answer.

Clinical AI that always answers creates liability. Teams need a gate that classifies every question before reasoning — and routes missing evidence to the right human.

01

Hallucination risk

Models invent values, medications, and scores when the record is silent. One confident wrong answer is worse than no answer.

02

Missing evidence

Fall-risk questions need instruments and vitals within the window. Without them, the honest move is gather — not guess.

03

Off-distribution asks

Billing, legal, and out-of-chart questions should abstain. Concept distance catches what keyword filters miss.

04

No audit trail

Every response should show PROCEED, GATHER, or ABSTAIN with why — so clinicians trust the workflow, not the prose.

How it works

Three outcomes.
One trusted record.

NextStep classifies every question before the model answers. Answers are grounded only when the gate says PROCEED.

01

PROCEED

Evidence is in-band and present. The model answers only from the patient record — with labeled sections and every medication named.

✓ in-distribution · vitals + fall-risk on file
02

GATHER

Question is clinical but data is missing or ambiguous. Name what's needed — Morse survey, PT test, or clinician clarification.

→ route to patient / PT / nurse
03

ABSTAIN

Off-distribution or unanswerable from the chart. Explain why — never fabricate a clinical conclusion.

✗ hand back to clinician
